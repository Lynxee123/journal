Friday started with something a little different. I was taken through the required Safety and WISHA walkthrough, which meant touring the building to locate all the exits, fire extinguishers, first aid kits, and everything else you hope you never actually need. It's not the most glamorous part of an internship, but it's genuinely good to know, especially in a healthcare environment where the stakes are a little higher than your average office.

There's also been a recurring issue with imaging requests getting routed to IT incorrectly, which apparently has happened enough times that a new policy is in the works to address it.

Oh, and there were Blizzards! As in the Dairy Queen kind. As in, the real highlight of the day. I did have to duck out early, but honestly, leaving on a Blizzard note is not a bad way to close out the week.
