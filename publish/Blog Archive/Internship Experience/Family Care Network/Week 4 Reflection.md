The Lynden installation day stuck with me as one of the best examples yet of how FCN handles complexity in the field. Between the PoE switch swap, the VLAN configurations, the passthrough cabling, and the trunk port issues, there were at least four distinct problems that all had to be solved in one location in one night. Week four made it clear that real IT work is less about knowing all the answers and more about knowing how to find them.
