We knocked out the last of the LFM phones today, three more boxes down and officially done with that batch. It feels good to close out a project that's been running in the background.

Though the last phone decided it wanted to go out with a little drama. It wasn't picking up an extension number, which is one of those problems that looks simple on the surface but can have a dozen different causes underneath. Ryan reached out to the phone company's support line to dig into it, and ultimately the fix was a factory reset. Sometimes the most straightforward solution is just wiping the slate clean and starting over. The phone cooperated after that, got its extension like nothing happened, and made it into the box with the rest of them.
