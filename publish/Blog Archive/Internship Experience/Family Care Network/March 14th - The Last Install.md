The last install... and the last day. 78 phones at NSFM on a bright, sunny Saturday morning with the whole team present.

It felt bittersweet in the way that good things ending always do. A little anticlimactic too, if I'm being honest. No grand finale, no send-off montage. Just another install, another set of phones finding their extensions, another clinic a little better connected than it was the day before. And maybe that's the point. The work doesn't pause for goodbyes. It just keeps going, and for a few months, I got to be part of that. :)
